Saturday, April 18

Full Adventure Day — Nature + FQF + Music

🌿 Choose Your Morning Adventure

  • Option A: Swamp Tour — 9:30 AM departure, back by 1 PM. See alligators, cypress swamps, Spanish moss. ~25 min drive from hotel. $35-60/person. April is prime season. Book: Cajun Pride (cajunprideswamptours.com) or Cajun Encounters.
  • Option B: City Park + Bayou St. John — Take the Canal St streetcar to City Park. Visit the Besthoff Sculpture Garden (FREE, 90+ sculptures in 11 acres). Then kayak Bayou St. John — Kayak-iti-Yat runs guided tours past historic homes.
  • Option C: Steamboat Natchez — Board at Toulouse St Wharf, 11 AM boarding, 11:30 AM departure. Jazz cruise on the Mississippi. $43.50/adult or $65 with Creole lunch. ~2 hours. Live jazz band on board.

Essentials & Tips

Everything you need to know

🎶 French Quarter Festival 2026

  • Dates: April 16–19 (Thu–Sun) — you're here for ALL of it
  • Hours: 11 AM – 8 PM daily
  • Cost: 100% FREE
  • Scale: 302 performances across 20 stages
  • Location: Throughout the French Quarter — Jackson Square, Woldenberg Riverfront Park, Bourbon St, Royal St, the Mint, new Governor Nicholls Wharf expansion
  • Must-see acts: PJ Morton, Irma Thomas, Big Freedia, Rebirth Brass Band, Dirty Dozen, The Soul Rebels, Kermit Ruffins, Preservation Brass, Dawn Richard
  • Tip: Download the official FQF app for real-time schedules

🚃 Getting Around

  • Streetcar: $1.25/ride or $9 three-day Jazzy Pass (unlimited). Get the GoMobile app.
  • St. Charles Line: Canal St → Garden District → Uptown → Audubon Park. ~45 min end-to-end. Runs 24 hrs.
  • Canal Street Line: Downtown → City Park / NOMA. Great for the Sculpture Garden.
  • Riverfront Line: Short scenic ride along the Mississippi.
  • Uber: Expect surge pricing during FQF. Streetcar + walking is often faster in the Quarter.

🌊 Mississippi River Experiences

  • Steamboat Natchez: Boards 11 AM & 2 PM daily at Toulouse St Wharf. Jazz cruise $43.50, with lunch $65. ~2 hrs.
  • Moon Walk: Free riverfront promenade behind Jackson Square. Great sunset spot.
  • Woldenberg Park: Riverfront park near Aquarium — also an FQF stage area.
  • Crescent Park: 1.4-mile elevated riverfront park in Bywater. "Rusty Rainbow" bridge entrance. Free.

🌿 Nature & Outdoors

  • Swamp Tour: Cajun Pride, 9:30 AM/12 PM/2:15 PM departures. ~25 min from hotel. $35-60. Book ahead for weekend.
  • City Park: Besthoff Sculpture Garden (FREE, 90+ sculptures). Botanical Garden. Big Lake kayaks/pedal boats.
  • Bayou St. John: Kayak tours through Mid-City. Kayak-iti-Yat runs guided tours.
  • Jean Lafitte Barataria Preserve: Free boardwalk trails through swamp. Near swamp tour launches.
  • Audubon Park: Via St. Charles streetcar. Live oak alleys, lagoons, zoo adjacent.
